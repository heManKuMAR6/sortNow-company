Case Studies

Real-world results from our data and AI consulting engagements

E-Commerce

Real-Time Recommendation Engine for $500M Retailer

The Problem

A major online retailer had a batch-based recommendation system that updated only once per day. This meant customers saw stale recommendations that didn't reflect recent browsing behavior, leading to poor conversion rates and missed revenue opportunities.

Our Solution

We built a real-time ML inference pipeline using Kafka, Redis, and custom-trained collaborative filtering models. The system processes 50,000 events per second and serves personalized recommendations with sub-50ms latency. We implemented A/B testing infrastructure to measure impact and continuously optimize models.

Impact
  • 23% increase in click-through rate on recommendations
  • $12M annual revenue lift from improved conversion
  • 15% reduction in infrastructure costs through optimization
  • 3-month time to production from project kickoff
Financial Services

Fraud Detection System for Fintech Startup

The Problem

A fast-growing fintech company was losing millions to fraud while also blocking legitimate transactions due to overly aggressive rules. Their legacy rule-based system couldn't adapt to evolving fraud patterns and had a 40% false positive rate.

Our Solution

We designed and deployed a machine learning-based fraud detection system using gradient boosting models with real-time feature engineering. The system analyzes 200+ signals per transaction and provides risk scores in under 100ms. We implemented automated model retraining to adapt to new fraud patterns.

Impact
  • 85% reduction in fraud losses (from $8M to $1.2M annually)
  • 60% decrease in false positives, improving customer experience
  • 99.97% uptime with automated failover and monitoring
  • ROI of 15x in the first year
Healthcare

Modern Data Warehouse for Healthcare Provider Network

The Problem

A network of 50+ healthcare facilities had patient data scattered across incompatible systems. Generating reports for compliance or operational insights required weeks of manual data extraction and reconciliation. Data quality issues led to billing errors and compliance risks.

Our Solution

We built a HIPAA-compliant data warehouse on Snowflake with automated ELT pipelines from all source systems. Implemented data quality frameworks, master data management, and self-service BI dashboards. Created a data governance framework with role-based access controls and audit logging.

Impact
  • From weeks to minutes for standard compliance reports
  • $3.2M recovered from previously unidentified billing errors
  • 100% data quality for critical patient safety metrics
  • Zero HIPAA violations since implementation
Manufacturing

Predictive Maintenance Platform for Industrial Equipment

The Problem

A manufacturing company faced frequent unplanned equipment downtime costing $50K per hour. Their preventive maintenance schedule was based on fixed intervals, leading to both unnecessary maintenance and unexpected failures. They had sensor data but no way to act on it.

Our Solution

We built an IoT data pipeline ingesting sensor data from 500+ machines and trained predictive models to forecast equipment failures 72 hours in advance. Created a monitoring dashboard for maintenance teams with automated alerts. Implemented edge computing for real-time anomaly detection.

Impact
  • 70% reduction in unplanned downtime
  • $8M annual savings from avoided downtime and optimized maintenance
  • 92% accuracy in predicting failures 72 hours in advance
  • 30% reduction in maintenance costs through optimization
SaaS

Cloud Migration & Cost Optimization for B2B SaaS

The Problem

A rapidly growing SaaS company was spending $400K/month on AWS with unpredictable costs and frequent performance issues. Their infrastructure was over-provisioned in some areas and under-provisioned in others. Deployments were manual and error-prone, taking 4+ hours.

Our Solution

We redesigned their cloud architecture with auto-scaling, containerization (EKS), and infrastructure-as-code (Terraform). Implemented comprehensive monitoring and cost allocation by customer. Built CI/CD pipelines for zero-downtime deployments. Optimized database queries and caching strategies.

Impact
  • 52% reduction in monthly AWS costs (from $400K to $192K)
  • 99.98% uptime vs. previous 99.5%
  • From 4 hours to 15 minutes for production deployments
  • 10x faster API response times through optimization
Logistics

Route Optimization ML System for Delivery Network

The Problem

A last-mile delivery company was using basic routing algorithms that didn't account for real-time traffic, driver preferences, or package priorities. This led to late deliveries, high fuel costs, and poor driver satisfaction. Manual route planning took 2 hours daily.

Our Solution

We built a machine learning-powered route optimization system that considers 50+ variables including traffic patterns, weather, driver skills, and delivery time windows. The system generates optimal routes in under 30 seconds and provides real-time re-routing based on changing conditions.

Impact
  • 28% reduction in total miles driven
  • $2.4M annual savings in fuel and vehicle maintenance
  • 95% on-time delivery rate (up from 78%)
  • 40% improvement in driver satisfaction scores
