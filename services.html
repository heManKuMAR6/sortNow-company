Our Services

Comprehensive solutions across the modern data and AI technology stack

Data Engineering & Warehousing

The Problem

Your data is scattered across multiple systems, making it impossible to get a unified view. Analytics queries are slow, data quality is inconsistent, and your team spends more time wrangling data than generating insights.

What We Build

We design and implement modern data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift) with robust ELT pipelines that consolidate data from all sources. Our solutions include data quality frameworks, automated testing, and optimized schemas for fast analytics.

The Outcome

A single source of truth for all business data, 10x faster queries, automated data quality checks, and self-service analytics capabilities for your teams. Typical projects reduce data processing costs by 40-60%.

ML Models to Production

The Problem

Your data scientists have built promising models in notebooks, but they're stuck in development. Moving to production is slow, risky, and requires extensive engineering effort. Models drift over time without monitoring.

What We Build

We build production ML infrastructure including feature stores, model serving APIs, batch prediction pipelines, and monitoring dashboards. We containerize models, set up A/B testing frameworks, and implement automated retraining workflows.

The Outcome

Models serving millions of predictions with sub-100ms latency, automated monitoring and alerting, seamless model updates without downtime, and clear ROI tracking. Reduce time-to-production from months to weeks.

MLOps & CI/CD for ML

The Problem

Your ML team lacks standardized processes. Model versions are tracked in spreadsheets, experiments aren't reproducible, and deploying updates is a manual, error-prone process. You can't answer "which model is in production right now?"

What We Build

We implement comprehensive MLOps platforms using tools like MLflow, Kubeflow, or custom solutions. This includes experiment tracking, model registry, automated testing, CI/CD pipelines, and production monitoring with drift detection.

The Outcome

Full visibility into all models and experiments, one-click deployments, automated rollback on performance degradation, and reproducible ML workflows. Teams ship 3-5x more model iterations with higher confidence.

Cloud Architecture & Migration

The Problem

Your infrastructure is either over-provisioned (wasting money) or under-provisioned (causing outages). You're locked into legacy systems or struggling with cloud complexity. Costs are unpredictable and scaling is painful.

What We Build

We design cloud-native architectures on AWS, GCP, or Azure optimized for your workload. This includes auto-scaling infrastructure, cost optimization strategies, disaster recovery plans, and migration roadmaps with zero-downtime transitions.

The Outcome

Infrastructure that scales automatically with demand, 30-50% reduction in cloud costs, 99.9%+ uptime, and complete infrastructure-as-code for reproducibility. Your team gains cloud expertise through hands-on collaboration.

AI/Data Technical Partnership

The Problem

You need ongoing technical leadership and execution capacity but can't justify full-time senior hires. Strategic initiatives stall due to lack of expertise. You need a trusted partner who understands your business and technology deeply.

What We Build

We provide fractional CTO/VP Engineering services with dedicated monthly capacity. This includes strategic planning, architecture reviews, hands-on development, team mentoring, and technology evaluation. Think of us as an extension of your leadership team.

The Outcome

Access to senior technical leadership at a fraction of full-time cost, continuous innovation pipeline, faster decision-making, and upskilled internal teams. Perfect for growth-stage companies building data/AI capabilities.

Resource Augmentation

The Problem

You have critical projects but lack the specialized talent to execute. Hiring is slow and risky. You need senior engineers who can contribute immediately without months of onboarding. Contractors often lack the quality you need.

What We Build

We embed senior data engineers, ML engineers, or cloud architects directly into your team. They work alongside your engineers, follow your processes, and contribute to your codebase. We provide the talent, you maintain full control.

The Outcome

Immediate acceleration of project timelines, knowledge transfer to your team, high-quality code that meets your standards, and flexibility to scale up or down as needed. Our engineers integrate seamlessly and deliver from day one.
